How To Play Connect N

You will take turns with the computer putting pieces into columns on the board. You will go first, and the computer will go second.

To choose the column you want to put a piece in, simply click the button above the column.

The goal of the game is to connect a certain number of pieces in a row. This can be achieved horizontally, vertically, and diagonally.

You may be wondering why the game is called "Connect N" as opposed to the classic "Connect 4". This is because there are 3 game modes: Small, Medium, and Large. The initial game mode is Medium. In order to change difficulty, navigate to the difficulty menu at the top left of the window.

Rules for each game mode:

For the Small mode, you need to connect 3 in a row to win. The board will be a 4x5 grid.

For the Medium mode, you need to connect 4 in a row to win. The board will be a 6x7 grid.

For the Large mode, you need to connect 5 in a row to win. The board will be an 8x9 grid.

Good luck!
